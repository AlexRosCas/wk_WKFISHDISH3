# ToR b: Towards SDM good practice guidelines

This directory contains the materials, datasets, and collaborative workspaces
associated with ToR b.

The objective of ToR b is to identify, evaluate, and develop good/best practices
for SDMs in fisheries and marine ecological applications. Particular emphasis is
placed on methodological challenges, practical implementation, uncertainty
quantification, and the interpretation of SDM outputs.

### `theme_1/` – `theme_5/`

Collaborative workspaces for the five ToR b theme groups.


These repositories may contain:

* analysis scripts,
* exploratory modelling exercises,
* figures and results,
* literature summaries,
* meeting notes,
* draft recommendations,
* example workflows,
* and other materials relevant to the theme.

Participants are encouraged to contribute code, ideas, and documentation
directly within the relevant theme repository.


## Suggested workflow

1. Explore the common case studies in `case_studies/`.
2. Apply and compare alternative modelling approaches.
3. Document findings, limitations, and recommendations within the relevant theme
   repository.
4. Synthesise outcomes into guidance, recommendations, and workshop deliverables.


## Principles

The purpose of these materials is not to identify a single "best" modelling
approach, but to:

* understand the strengths and limitations of different methods,
* identify situations where particular approaches perform well or poorly,
* improve transparency and reproducibility,
* and develop practical guidance for future SDM applications.
